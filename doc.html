Intro

RofloSvin is a friendly Discord bot with a bunch of fun and useful features for your server!

Short review of what Svin can do:

  • Main Feature: Enabled Artificial Intelligence right in the bot. Just write s! [YOUR REQUEST] and your query will be answered directly in Discord!
  • Music: Include music from YouTube (then it is probably possible to update and add music from other resources such as Spotify, SoundCloud, etc.) for your friends and feel like a cool and crazy DJ!
  • Responses: You can mention or reply to the bot's messages and it can answer you something funny. Also, you can ask the bot something and it will always answer you!
  • Games:Play in Discord in 3 games to choose from, if you want to play a game where you can fight the enemy I advise you to play Shooter game, if you want to play a text game in which you can choose different developments and endings, if you argue with friends to play igur Heads and Tails.
  • Help Button: If you have problems using our bot, you can write a special command !help and the bot will give you a URL to solve your issues.
